Fire Problem

  • Bushfires are an inconsistent hazard in Geelong City but it does create havoc when it affect the city. With high risk fire regions as close as 80km from Geelong, it is important to predict the possibility of fire in each Geelong and nearby suburb. Use time series prediction algorithms like LSTM, GRU and boosting algorithms​​ This visual shows an overview of all suburbs close to Geelong classified as Fire (In Orange) or No-Fire (In Blue). Users will be notified in case their suburb is Fire-Prone on a particular day if they have subscribed to our mailing list.

    This the predicted outcome for our best model as on 3rd January, 2020. We see that all regions prone to fire on that day have been identified. Reference Link
